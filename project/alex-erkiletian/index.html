Alex Erkiletian Letterpress Stationary

The appreciation of fine writing instruments & driving 100mph.

A client with blind approval, no pressure.

The creative brief I received for this project was simple:
– Alex needs a personal stationey package.
– He is bold, drives a suped-up black Ford Mustang and collects japanese wood cuts.
– He does not need to see anything prior to printing, just design, print and deliver it.
– He trusts you.
I delivered a beautiful letter pressed stationery package. Won a Gold Addy. And received a phone call from the client full of praise.
